Milestone Points (MP) Rewards

[1 MP] Clicks are multiplied by 1.60x.
[2 MP] Clicks are boosted by +25% per owned MP.
[3 MP] Unlocks Quantum Energy passive generation (+1 QE/s).
[5 MP] Atoms gain is raised to the power of 1.10.
[8 MP] Click Level boosts all sub-resources by +2% per level.
[12 MP] Covalent, Ionic, and Metallic bond costs are reduced by 10%.
[16 MP] Unlocks 2 new high-tier click milestones (at 50Sp and 200Sp).
[20 MP] Quantum Energy passive gain is raised to the power of 1.15.
[28 MP] Unlocks 3 advanced click milestones (at 1Oc, 5Oc, and 20Oc).
[35 MP] All sub-resources together multiply click gain by (Atoms * Energy * Power * Matter) ^ 0.005.
[42 MP] Unlock Resource Mini-Milestones:
  • Atoms >= 1B: +25% Energy Gain
  • Energy >= 10M: +25% Power Gain
  • Power >= 100k: +25% Matter Gain
  • Matter >= 1k: +25% Molecules Gain
[50 MP] Softcap penalty is heavily reduced (from exponent ^0.80 to ^0.90).
